Overview

CareerConnect is a unified, full-stack campus placement and internship tracking platform designed to connect Training & Placement Officers (TPOs), company recruiters, and student candidates into a single, cohesive placement ecosystem.

By automating vacancy notices, status progressive pipelines, inline PDF resume viewing, and instant trigger logs, CareerConnect addresses communication delays and manual admin work, establishing a modern digital placement workflow.
